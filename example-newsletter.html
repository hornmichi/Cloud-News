5 Essential Tips for Cloud Cost Optimization

Tips & Tricks Normal By David Kim - Cloud Architect
Learn practical strategies to reduce your cloud infrastructure costs while maintaining performance and reliability.
Cloud cost optimization is crucial for any organization using cloud services. Here are 5 essential tips to help you reduce costs while maintaining performance: 1. **Right-size your instances**: Regularly review and adjust instance sizes based on actual usage patterns. 2. **Use reserved instances**: Commit to 1-3 year terms for predictable workloads to save 30-60%. 3. **Implement auto-scaling**: Scale resources up and down automatically based on demand. 4. **Monitor and tag resources**: Use proper tagging to track costs and identify optimization opportunities. 5. **Leverage spot instances**: Use spot instances for non-critical workloads to save up to 90%. By implementing these strategies, you can significantly reduce your cloud costs while maintaining the performance your applications need.
